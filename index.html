About Us

Know About Our Company & Our History

Kashmir Career And Counseling Pvt Ltd.Popularly known as KCC, Kashmir's Educational Services started as a small entrepreneurial venture in 2018 with the vision of spreading educational awareness and providing admission guidance to students based on aptitude, Interest and personality. Our Signature services include Career Counselling, Admission Guidance, and Institutional Promotion. We are a pioneer of Online Career and Counseling, first of its kind ever used in Kashmir for Career Counselling of students, based on which most suitable career path are advised to students.
